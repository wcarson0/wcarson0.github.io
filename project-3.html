Project Overview

Over the past year, I embarked on a fascinating project that delved into the realm of AI art and multimedia creation. It all began when OpenAI's chatGPT gained widespread attention, inspiring me to explore the possibilities of their chatGPT API through Huggingface. Intrigued by the concept of generating art from text, I experimented with their 'Dall-E 2' software, crafting captivating text-to-image artworks. However, my quest for even greater potential led me to Midjourney, a text-to-image creator highly regarded by the online AI community. Yet, I yearned to push the boundaries further and stumbled upon a game-changing Google Colab notebook called VQGAN-Clip, enabling text-to-video transformations. Eager to enhance my workflow, I switched to utilizing the Deforum Stable Diffusion Google-Colab notebook, after discovering its superior features within an AI art Discord community. Another noteworthy tool in my repertoire is so-vits-svc, an open-source software developed on GitHub, which empowers users to train their own AI models to speak in various voices and languages.

Leveraging this software, I've created captivating music videos featuring artists singing songs outside their usual repertoireâ€”a viral sensation on social media today. My creative process typically involves generating a series of still images using Midjourney's text-to-image capabilities. Subsequently, I select a song or create a cover using so-vits-svc, meticulously synchronizing the timing of the music with the desired number of frames per text-to-video sequence based on the initial image batch. By producing two versions of each picture and expertly blending them, I achieve seamless transitions within the AI-generated videos. To bring it all together, I rely on DaVinci Resolve, a powerful video editing software, to craft the final composition. This project represents my relentless pursuit of innovation at the intersection of AI and artistic expression.
